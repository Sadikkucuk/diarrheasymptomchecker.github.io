How this works

Inputs are converted to feet. Volume = area × depth. Results show cubic yards (yd³), cubic feet (ft³), and cubic meters (m³). Bag count = ft³ / selected bag size. If pricing is filled: bulk cost = yd³ × price; bags cost = bag count × price. All math is done on your Cloudflare Worker.
