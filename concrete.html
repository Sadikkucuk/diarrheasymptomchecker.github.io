Concrete Volume Calculator prototype

Calculate concrete volume for Square/Rectangle slab, Round slab, Wall, Footer, Square & Round columns, Steps, and Curb & Gutter. Choose units (in, ft, cm, m). Optionally estimate cost.

1) Select shape & enter dimensions

2) Results

How this works

We convert all inputs to feet, compute volume according to the chosen shape, then report in ft³, yd³, and m³ (1 yd³ = 27 ft³, 1 m³ ≈ 35.3147 ft³). If a unit price is entered, cost = volume in the selected cubic unit × price. All math runs on a Cloudflare Worker.
