Toothache Cause Estimator prototype

Educational demo — not medical advice. Red flags: facial swelling with fever, difficulty swallowing or breathing, spreading redness, trismus (cannot open mouth), chest pain with jaw pain on exertion, or severe pain 2–5 days after extraction. Seek urgent care for red flags.

How this works

We compare your selections to typical symptom sets from medical encyclopedias. Each cause gets a score = matches ÷ that cause’s total listed symptoms, normalized to 100%. This is a naive overlap metric — not a diagnosis.
