Cyanosis Cause Estimator prototype

Educational demo — not medical advice. Emergency red flags: sudden severe shortness of breath, chest pain, confusion, very low oxygen reading (<90%), inability to speak in full sentences, choking/stridor, blue lips/tongue at rest. Seek urgent care for red flags.

How this works

We compare your selections to typical symptom sets from medical encyclopedias. Each cause gets a score = matches ÷ that cause’s total listed symptoms, normalized to 100%. This is a naive overlap metric — not a diagnosis.
