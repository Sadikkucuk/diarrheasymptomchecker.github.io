Anemia Cause Estimator prototype

Educational demo — not medical advice. Emergency red flags: chest pain, shortness of breath at rest, fainting, very low blood pressure, black tarry stools, vomiting blood, rapidly worsening jaundice or dark urine. Seek urgent care for red flags.

How this works

We compare your selections to typical symptom sets from medical encyclopedias. Each cause gets a score = matches ÷ that cause’s total listed symptoms, normalized to 100%. This is a naive overlap metric — not a diagnosis.
